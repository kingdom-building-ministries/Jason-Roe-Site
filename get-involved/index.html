Bio

Jason's ministry started on the front lines of inner-city youth ministry in 1994. After several years of youth ministry and pastoring, Jason launched into full-time evangelism. In 2005 Jason founded JC Evangelistic Ministries, where he currently serves as President. He is also an Itinerant speaker for Kingdom Building Ministries. He is a graduate of Asbury Theological Seminary. Jason is blessed with a wonderful wife, Holly, who is a partner in his ministry. They are the parents of three kids, Madison, Grayson and Lawson. They are dedicated to the lifelong mission of spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ throughout the world.

Would you like to join Jason in ministry?

There are several ways you can get involved:

  • You can join Jason’s prayer force. There are people all over the world that shower Jason’s ministry with prayer.
  • You can join Jason on the mission field on one of his short term mission trips to Zimbabwe or Honduras.
  • You can join Jason and other Kingdom Building Ministry Speakers at one of our discipleship training events like 16 Days, The Experience, or Deep Camp.
  • You can join Jason by giving a financial gift to help him to continue preaching and ministering to others. This also enables Jason and his family to continue their journey in this faith ministry.
