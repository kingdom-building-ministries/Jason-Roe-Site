Video Description

Jason Roe is a passionate communicator of the Gospel. He brings a compelling style of preaching that uses humor and relational stories to communicate the message of Christ in a real and relevant way. He desires to help local churches become vital congregations while challenging and equipping individuals to fulfill their God-given mission. God has gifted Jason with the ability to break through generational barriers, to communicate with clarity and passion to people of all ages. The foundation of Jason's ministry claims Mark 16:15: ‘Go into all the world and proclaim the Gospel to the whole creation.’
