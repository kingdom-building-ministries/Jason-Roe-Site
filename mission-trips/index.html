Zimbabwe Missions

For over a decade, Jason has been leading mission teams to Zimbabwe. Each year he assembles a team of believers to go and work alongside local churches, helping them to be the beacon of hope in their communities. The teams use medical and vision clinics, Bible School, church construction and crusades to use the gifts of each team member and to touch the lives of many. Annually, Jason is seeking doctors, nurses, teachers, preachers, contractors and faithful disciples who are willing to pour out their hearts into the lives of others. The mission typically takes place in late May and is open to believers that are over the age of 18, unless accompanied by a parent.
